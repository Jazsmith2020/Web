About me...

Makeup Tips

What helps for gel liner? I use a product called Inglot Duraline. You can mix a drop of it with any eyeshadow or gel liner to make it a water proof gel liner.

To have matte finish look. When applying your foundation, dab where you are putting it, then spray your matte setting spray before you blend the fooundation on to your skin. This will help set your foundation, making your skin look matte.

When doing your eyebrows...use a brush and gel, brush up your eyebrows, the draw a line above where you want your eye brows, then draw a line below your brow. Once you have the shape of your eyebrow. Fill in your brow with the gel. Brush it up and how you want your eye brow. Done!
