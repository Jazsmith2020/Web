I am Me

I am me is a story about my life. Growing up gay, transitioning and finding myself through love, beauty and art. It was made to inspire others to never give up and to bring awareness for transgender people. That we are here, and we can live a normal life, what society considers normal. I am me has won two awards from 2 separate film festvals, one for best documentary and another for the audience award. It has had a shout out in Vogue Magazine Canada, and has been screened at Berlinale in Berlin, ImagineNative in Toronto, Red Nation Film Festival in Los Angeles, Vancouver Queer Film festival as well as the Skoden Film festival in Vancouver. It was been screened in many film festivals throughout the world, which I am so so grateful for. Finding my voice, people listening and appreciating my story.
